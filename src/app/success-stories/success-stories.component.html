Ashley was rushed to the hospital by her boyfriend, after he found her having an unexpected seizure. After several tests, the doctors found that she had a tumor on her brain. She had her first brain surgery on July 1, 2020.

On 7/14/2020, Ashley was diagnosed with a Grade 3 Astrocytoma - a malignant brain tumor.

11/19/2020, Ashley rang the bell this morning to commemorate the end of her radiation treatments. For the first time since July 1st; Ashley will be drug/chemical/steroid/radiation FREE for the next 28 days or so!
