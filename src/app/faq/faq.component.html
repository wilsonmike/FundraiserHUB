How do I enroll a beneficiary and set up a store?

You can submit our form below or on the homepage, after we recieve your information we will contact you regarding next steps.

Does it cost anything to set up a store?

Nope! We can build your campaign with no cost to you. $10 of every shirt purchased goes directly to your beneficiary to help them during their troubling times.

How can my family and friends find the store?

Once you approve the t-shirt artwork, we will have a dedicated domain and store invdividually made for you. We also provide email and social media templates that can be tailored with the link to your site.

Where are your products manufactured and customized?

Blank products are ordered from a variety of distributors and manufacturers located in the USA and each product has a label indicating its origin. All customization and decorating is done in our facility located in Commerce Township, Michigan.
